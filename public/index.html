About Us

Our Vision &
Mission

SCC is a non-stock, non-profit educational institution that envisions itself to be a Center of Excellence in Academics, Technology, and the Arts. It aspires to produce professionals and leaders who are globally competitive, imbued with Christian values, integrity, patriotism and stewardship.

Through quality human education, St. Cecilia's College commits itself to cultivating Christian values, providing knowledge and skills, and fostering love for country and service to fellowmen.
