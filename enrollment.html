CONDITION OF PAYMENT
A. MODE OF PAYMENT

The tuition and other school fees shall be payable in cash in 4 monthly installments in accordance with the following schedule.

1st payment
payable on or before
Prelim Exam
2nd payment
payable on or before
Midterm Exam
3rd payment
payable on or before
Prefinal Exam
4th payment
due on or before
Final Exam

In case of failure to pay at least two (2) installments of the due and demandable tuition and other school fees as indicated in the above schedule of payment on installments, we (the parents) or I (in case of student) shall recognize, without reservation, the right/authority of the school to drop me (or our child) from the school (or suspend me or our child) from further attending classes and enter the school campus, without need of judicial intervention or permission. That I, (or our child) shall be re-admitted only as soon as the tuition and other school fees are paid PROVIDED, however, that I, (our child) will be solely responsible in keeping up with the lessons and assignments missed and shall not have any right to require that special lessons, quizzes and or examinations be given to make-up for the quizzes and/or examinations given during the school day (our child was not allowed to enter and attend classes).

B. Policy on withdrawals and refund

The date of filling the notice or withdrawal shall be considered the date of withdrawal and shall the basis in determining the amount to be charged for the term. Computation for adjustment will be in the accounting Office.

  1. Enrollment deposited can be refunded subject to the following conditions.
    1. 80% refund before the subjects are encoded and before the enrollment closes.
    2. 20% refund if the subjects are encoded before the enrollment closes.
    3. No refund after the enrollment closes.
  2. 10% charge on tuition and other school fees if a student withdraws within the first week of classes regardless of whether or not he/she actually attended classes.
  3. 20% charge on tuition and other school fees if a student withdraws within the second week of classes regardless of whether or not he/she actually attended classes.
  4. 50% charge on tuition and other school fees if a student withdraws within the third week of classes regardless of whether or not he/she actually attended classes.
  5. 75% charge on tuition and other school fees if a student withdraws within the fourth week of classes regardless of whether or not he/she actually attended classes.
  6. 100% charge on tuition and other school fees if a student withdraws within the fifth week of classes regardless of whether or not he/she actually attended classes.
